Chocolate Surprise Milkshake

Description

The lasagna take time and work, but remenber: "No pain, no game"

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons milk
  • 3 scoops chocolate ice cream
  • 2 tablespoons chocolate syrup
  • 5 tablespoons powdered chocolate drink mix
  • 5 cubes ice
  • Â¼ cup chocolate chips

How to Make Chocolate Surprise Milkshake

  1. In a blender, combine milk, ice cream, chocolate syrup,
    chocolate drink mix, ice cubes, and chocolate chips.
  2. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.
